First three months with a newborn

  • Have lightning over the chanching table to give your baby something to focus on besided dressing and diaper changing
  • Use pacifier directly to prevent working on it for months
  • Noticing that your little one does it's needs everytime you removed the diaper? It's because it gets cold and their body tries to remove any access to have less to heat up. Try opening the diaper then close it again and you won't have to change as often
  • Open the body, wrap it up around the arms and close it above the shoulder for a still baby during changing
